Products

Notecards

Blank 5" x 7" note cards with prints of selected paintings are available.  The image is printed on matte photo paper mounted on heavy weight card stock. A matching envelope is included.

 

Prints

Ready to frame prints of selected paintings are available on watercolor paper, mounted on foam core with an antique white mat. Acid free materials and archival quality inks are used.

Small prints are about 8" x 10", matted to fit a 11" x 14" frame.  Large prints are about 11" x 14", matted to fit a 16" x 20" frame. 

Note: Exact dimensions of prints depend on proportions of the original painting.

Original Oil Paintings

Original oil paintings have a matte black metal frame and are priced as indicated on this web site.

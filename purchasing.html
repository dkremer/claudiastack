Purchasing

Please contact me via email if you are interested in purchasing an original oil painting listed for sale on this site. Please identify the painting you are interested in, provide your shipping address and information on how to contact you with a total price that includes shipping and sales tax if applicable.

Payment will be handled through PayPal and the painting will be shipped USPS Priority Mail.
